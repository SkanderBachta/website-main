FINCRO 06/2021 - 08/2021 Python | R | RSTUDIO | RSHINY

Reserve and VAP for a life annuity

The objective of this work was to calculate the life annuity of a cohort of Italian insureds composed of 60% women and 40% men born in 1955.to do this we made a study and analysis of the mortality rate among Italians in the first chapter then the implementation of the Lee-Carter model. The Lee-Carter model is a two-dimensional model that takes into account both the year and the age to determine the mortality rates, in order to predict the mortality rates for future years.

Finally, the last chapter is devoted to the construction of mortality tables for both sexes in order to calculate the probable present value of the contract for the cohorts born in 1955 contracted in 2015 -2045.
